# Postmortem Template

This document summarizes a production incident for stakeholders and internal learning.

The goal is transparency, alignment, and improvement.

---

## Incident Overview

- What happened?
- Who was affected?
- For how long?
- What was the customer impact?

---

## Resolution Summary

- How was the issue mitigated?
- Was rollback used?
- Was a hotfix deployed?

Keep explanation clear and non-technical where possible.

---

## Root Cause (Summary)

High-level explanation of the underlying cause.

Avoid blame. Focus on system gaps.

---

## What Went Well

- Fast detection?
- Clear communication?
- Effective rollback?
- Good monitoring?

Highlight strengths to reinforce good practices.

---

## What Can Be Improved

- Earlier detection?
- Clearer release validation?
- Stronger automation?
- Better documentation?

Be specific.

---

## Action Items

List of improvements with:

- Owner
- Priority
- Expected completion

---

## Follow-up Review

- Date of follow-up check
- Confirmation that action items were completed
- Verification that similar risk is reduced
